Our Story

A small café with a big obsession.

At Kugon Cafe, we believe every cup tells a story. We carefully source quality beans and craft each drink with intention — whether you're working, studying, or simply taking a breath.

Mission

To serve exceptional coffee that's traceable, ethical, and worth slowing down for — one cup, one neighbor at a time.

Vision

A café that feels like a second living room — where craft, community, and a really good espresso shot all show up daily.

Quality

Beans roasted weekly. Milk steamed to 60°C. Water filtered to spec. The small stuff is everything.
